For your safety and hygiene, according to internationally-accepted standards, the permitted clothing materials have to be made of 100% nylon or synthetic materials.

Why dress code?

Our amusement park has several rides to have fun on. Almost all of them involve motion or moving parts that require a special dress code to ensure your health and safety. For instance, loose garments like sarees, salwar, dupatta, churidar, shawls or scarves could get entangled and lead to potential harm to yourselves and others. Please ensure to avoid loose garments while enjoying the land rides for every one's safety.

In order to ensure that the water quality for all rides is maintained, we enforce a strict dress code.

The appropriate outfits for our water rides are T-shirts, shorts, bermudas, three-fourths, swimsuits.The permitted clothing materials are made of 100% nylon or synthetic material.

EZ-Wrist Band

EZ pay bands is an e-wallet, so you do not have to carry cash around inside the park. For example, if you are a group of 4, deposit cash of about2,000 at the EZ pay counter, and you will be given a wristband with that amount. After you keep all your belongings into a paid locker, you can then enjoy the park tension free. Pay for food, gift shop purchases, photographs, etc by simply using the wristband. You will get receipts for every purchase.When ready to leave, visit the EZ pay counter to get the balance cash remaining on your wristband
